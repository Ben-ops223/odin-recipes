Pizza Recipe

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 1/3 cups warm water (about 110°F or 43°C)
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • packet 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour or bread flour (spooned and leveled), plus extra for dusting
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp cornmeal (for dusting the pan)

For the Sauce & Toppings:

  • 1 cup store-bought or homemade pizza sauce
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil (for brushing the crust)
  • Optional: fresh basil, pepperoni, or minced garlic

Instructions

  1. Make the Dough

    Whisk the warm water, sugar, and yeast together in a large mixing bowl or stand mixer. Let it sit for 5 minutes until the yeast is frothy. Add the flour, olive oil, and salt. Mix until the dough pulls away from the sides of the bowl.

  2. Knead & Rise

    Knead the dough in a stand mixer or on a lightly floured surface for 5 minutes until soft and stretchy. Form the dough into a ball and place it in a lightly oiled bowl, turning to coat all sides. Cover and let rise in a warm place for 60 to 90 minutes, or until doubled in size.

  3. Preheat & Shape

    Preheat your oven to 475°F (246°C) and let it heat up for 15 minutes. Punch down your risen dough and divide it in half. Lightly grease a pizza pan or baking sheet and sprinkle it with cornmeal. Use your hands to gently stretch and flatten the dough out to the edges of the pan, pressing a raised rim along the edges.

  4. Top & Bake

    Brush the crust with olive oil and poke gentle dimples into the surface of the dough with your fingers to prevent large air bubbles. Spread an even layer of pizza sauce, followed by mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.
