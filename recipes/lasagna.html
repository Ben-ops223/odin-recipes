Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Oven-Ready Lasagna Noodles: 6 uncooked sheets.
  • Ground Italian Sausage: 1 pound (or ground beef).
  • asta Sauce: One 24-ounce jar of your favorite marinara.
  • Ricotta Cheese: 1 cup.
  • Mozzarella Cheese: 2 cups, shredded.

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 375ºF (190ºC).
  2. Brown the meat: Cook the sausage in a large pan over medium heat until no longer pink, then drain any excess grease.
  3. Combine the sauce: Stir the entire jar of pasta sauce directly into the pan with the browned sausage.
  4. Layer the base: Pour 1 cup of the meat sauce mixture into the bottom of an 11x7-inch casserole dish.
  5. Add first pasta layer: Top the sauce with 3 uncooked oven-ready lasagna noodles.
  6. Spread the cheese: Spread 1 cup of ricotta cheese evenly across the noodles, then sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella cheese over the top.
  7. Add second sauce layer: Spread another cup of meat sauce over the cheese layer.
  8. Repeat pasta and sauce: Place the remaining 3 uncooked noodles on top, and coat them completely with the rest of the meat sauce.
  9. Bake covered: Wrap the dish tightly with aluminum foil and bake for 50 minutes.
  10. Melt the top: Remove the foil, sprinkle the remaining 1 cup of mozzarella cheese over the top, and bake uncovered for 5 more minutes until bubbling.
  11. Rest and serve: Let the lasagna rest out of the oven for 10 minutes so it holds its shape perfectly when sliced.
