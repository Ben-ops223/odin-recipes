Burger Recipe

Ingredients (Makes 4 burgers):

  • Meat: 500 g ground beef (80/20 fat ratio)
  • Binder: 1 large egg
  • Seasoning: 1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce, 1 tsp. onion powder, salt, and black pepper to taste
  • Assembly: 4 brioche buns, sliced cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, and your favorite condiments

Instructions:

  1. Mix: Gently mix the beef, egg, Worcestershire sauce, and dry spices in a bowl. Do not overwork the meat, or the burger will turn out dense.
  2. Form: Shape the mixture into 4 equal patties. Make a slight indentation (dimple) in the center of each patty so they stay flat while cooking.
  3. Cook: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side.
  4. Melt: Add a slice of cheese on top during the last minute of cooking, covering the pan to trap the steam.
  5. Serve: Toast the buns, add your spreads, and serve immediately.
